Skiez Data Entry Services:


Simplifying Data Management
Data entry plays a crucial role in the success of businesses today, but it can be time-consuming and requires meticulous attention to detail. Skiez Technologies is here to support businesses in overcoming these challenges with our customized data entry services.

As a global leader in data entry services, Skiez understands the unique needs of our clients and offers top-notch solutions to meet them. Here are some of the specific data entry services we provide:

1. Scanning and Uploading Data:
We process, scan, and securely upload input files into the server through File Transfer Protocol (FTP) or Virtual Private Network (VPN) access to the customer's onsite network.

2. Downloading and Accessing Files:
Our experienced team downloads and accesses the documents, conducting a thorough review based on pre-defined work file criteria.

3. Key-in Document:
Downloaded documents are meticulously keyed in using specific web applications or MS-Excel, and then carefully verified for errors and duplicates.

4. Indexing, Sorting, and Moving:
The verified data is indexed, sorted, and organized in pre-determined folders for easy accessibility and streamlined usage.

5. Quality Checking:
All data entry projects undergo a rigorous quality check to ensure that accuracy levels meet the expected 99% quality standards before final delivery.

6. File Uploading:
Checked files are securely uploaded using FTP technology for further customer review and seamless collaboration.
