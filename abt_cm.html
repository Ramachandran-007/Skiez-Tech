Skiez Technologies

Skiez Technologies India Private Limited was established on April 10, 2013, by Dr. S. Davidraj, an Environmentalist, Wildlife enthusiast, and Social reformer. The company was officially incorporated on October 28, 2013. Since his early days, the founder has always had a compassionate inclination towards the underprivileged.

With the aim of providing employment opportunities to educated youths in Indian villages, the founder carefully selected Mr. D. Oswald Smith as the Managing Director. Mr. Smith holds an Engineering Degree in Information Technology, followed by a Masters degree in Business Management from the renowned Indian Institute of Planning Management. To support and advise the Managing Director, R. Regila, a devout woman, serves as a Director.

Skiez Technologies India Private Limited was incorporated as a private company on October 28, 2013, and is registered with the Registrar of Companies in Chennai. Classified as a non-governmental company, it has an authorized share capital of Rs. 100,000 and a paid-up capital of Rs. 100,000. The company is engaged in software publishing, which encompasses the production, supply, and documentation of non-customized software, including operating systems, business applications, and computer games for various platforms.
